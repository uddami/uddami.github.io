Our Journey

History & Founding

UYNBD was founded in 2019 by a group of passionate young Bangladeshis who believed that organized, principled youth action could transform their communities and nation.

2019
The Beginning

UYNBD founded in Dhaka by 12 co-founders with a vision to create Bangladesh's most impactful youth network.

2020
COVID Response

Mobilized 500+ volunteers for community relief during the pandemic, establishing UYNBD as a trusted humanitarian force.

2022
First Youth Summit

Inaugural Uddami Youth Summit attracted 800+ participants, setting the foundation for an annual flagship event.

2024
National Recognition

Signed MoU with Ministry of Youth and Sports; expanded to all 64 districts of Bangladesh.
